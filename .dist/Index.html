About Me

Hi, I'm Sheetal Gautam, a Website and Graphic Designer from India. I specialize in creating user-friendly, responsive websites and engaging graphics, blending creativity with technical expertise. With experience as a Web Developer at Afame Technologies, Graphic Designer at GROGLOBALLY, and Cloud Computing Intern at Encriptix, along with internships at Internslite and Yhills, I've honed my skills in web and graphic design, and game development. Currently, I'm pursuing Aerospace Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, which enhances my analytical approach to design. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate on innovative projects. Feel free to reach out to me at contact@exp.com or connect on LinkedIn.
